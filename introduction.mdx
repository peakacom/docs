## What type of user are you?

### 🧩 Business User

Analysts, operations teams, and non-technical users who want to connect data sources, analyze data, and send their consolidated data to a BI tool for visualization.

<Card href="/integrations" title="Integrations">
  Connect to CRMs, databases, and SaaS tools like Google Sheets, Meta Ads,
  QuickBooks, Shopify, and more.
</Card>

<Card href="/bi-tools" title="BI Tools">
  Send your datasets to data visualization tools such as Power BI, Tableau, or
  Google Looker Studio with just a few clicks.
</Card>
