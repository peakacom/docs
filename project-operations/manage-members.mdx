---
title: "Manage Members"
description: "Learn how to manage project members in Peaka."
---

This article explains how you can manage project members in Peaka.

### Opening manage members modal

1. Click on the three-dots button (ellipsis icon) associated with the project and click on the "**Manage Project Members**" button.

<img
  src="https://cdn.peaka.com/project-operations/click-3-dot.png"
  alt="open context menu of app view"
/>

That's it. You can see the manage members modal.

<img
  src="https://cdn.peaka.com/project-operations/manage-members-1.png"
  alt="manage members modal"
/>

### Add new member to the project

1. Click on the "Add Member" button on the top right corner of the modal.

2. Select the member or group from the dropdown menu and click on the "Add" button.

<img
  src="https://cdn.peaka.com/project-operations/manage-members-2.png"
  alt="add member modal"
/>

That's it. The member will be added to the project. You can see the member on the list of members.

<img
  src="https://cdn.peaka.com/project-operations/manage-members-3.png"
  alt="added member to the project"
/>

### Updating member's or group's role

1. Click on the role of the member or group to open the role selection dropdown and select the new role.

2. Select the new role from the dropdown.

<img
  src="https://cdn.peaka.com/project-operations/manage-members-4.png"
  alt="update member role"
/>

That's it. The member's role will be updated.

### Remove member or group from the project

1. Click on the three-dots button (ellipsis icon) associated with the member or group and click on the "Remove" option.

2. Click on the "Remove" button from the dropdown menu.

<img
  src="https://cdn.peaka.com/project-operations/manage-members-4.png"
  alt="remove member or group"
/>

That's it. The member or group will be removed from the project.
