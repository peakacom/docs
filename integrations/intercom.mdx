This article guides you through the process of connecting Intercom to Peaka, allowing you to seamlessly access your Intercom data within the Peaka platform.

## Prerequisites

Before you start, you will need the following information from Intercom.

- Client ID (required for advanced authentication)
- Client Secret (required for advanced authentication)

Follow the steps below to obtain it:

1. To obtain the Client ID and Client Secret, refer to this [article](https://developers.intercom.com/docs/build-an-integration/learn-more/authentication/setting-up-oauth#get-the-authorization-code).

## Connecting Intercom to Peaka

1. Navigate to the data model page in Peaka.

2. Click the "Bring your data" button to initiate the connection process.

3. In the modal that appears, select "Intercom" as your connection type.

4. Enter a name for your connection and input the Client ID and Client Secret you obtained in the previous step.

<img src="https://cdn.peaka.com/integrations/intercom-1.png" />

5. Click "Sign" to create your Intercom connection and allow Peaka access to your Intercom account.

Congratulations! You've successfully connected Intercom to Peaka. You can now access and manage your Intercom data seamlessly within the Peaka platform.
