This article guides you through the process of connecting Youtube Analytics to Peaka, allowing you to seamlessly access your Youtube Analytics data within the Peaka platform.

## Prerequisites

Before you start, you will need the following information from Youtube Analytics.

- Client ID (required for advanced authentication)
- Client Secret (required for advanced authentication)

Follow the steps below to obtain it:

1. To obtain the Client ID and Client Secret, refer to this [article](https://developers.google.com/identity/protocols/oauth2#1.-obtain-oauth-2.0-credentials-from-the-dynamic_data.setvar.console_name-.).

## Connecting Youtube Analytics to Peaka

Now that you have obtained the necessary integration data and credentials, you can connect Youtube Analytics to Peaka using the following steps:

1. Navigate to the data model page in Peaka.

2. Click the "Bring your data" button to initiate the connection process.

3. In the modal that appears, select "Youtube Analytics" as your connection type.

4. Enter a name for your connection, input the credentials you obtained in the previous steps.

<img src="https://cdn.peaka.com/integrations/youtube-analytics-1.png" />

5. Click "Sign in" to create your Youtube Analytics connection and allow the connection to access your Youtube Analytics data.

Congratulations! You've successfully connected Youtube Analytics to Peaka. You can now access and manage your Youtube Analytics data seamlessly within the Peaka platform.
