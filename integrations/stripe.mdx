This article guides you through the process of connecting Stripe to Peaka, allowing you to seamlessly access your Stripe data within the Peaka platform.

## Prerequisites

Before you start, you will need an API Key from Stripe.

Follow the steps below to obtain it:

1. Go to this [link](https://stripe.com/) and log in to your Stripe account. This will take you to your Stripe dashboard.

2. Click the "Developers" button, as shown in the image below.

<img src="images/integrations/stripe-1.png" />

3. In the Developers section, click the "API Keys" button.Then click the "Reveal test key" button to display your API Key.

<img src="images/integrations/stripe-2.png" />

## Connecting Stripe to Peaka

Now that you have your requirements, you can connect it to Peaka using the following steps:

1. Navigate to the settings page in Peaka.

2. Click the "Add First Connection" button to initiate the connection process.

3. In the modal that appears, select "Stripe" as your connection type.

4. Enter your connection name and API Key.

<img src="images/integrations/stripe-3.png" />

5. Click "Authorize" to create your Stripe connection.

<img src="images/integrations/stripe-4.png" />

Congratulations! You've successfully created a connection between Stripe and Peaka. You can now access and manage your Stripe data seamlessly within the Peaka platform.
