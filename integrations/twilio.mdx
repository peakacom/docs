This article guides you through the process of connecting Twilio to Peaka, allowing you to seamlessly access your Twilio data within the Peaka platform.

## Prerequisites

Before you start, you will need SID and Auth Token from Twilio.

Follow the steps below to obtain it:

1. Go to this [link](https://console.twilio.com/).

2. Scroll down on the page, and you will find your Twilio SID and Auth Token, as shown in the image below.

<img src="https://cdn.peaka.com/integrations/twilio-1.png" />

## Connecting Twilio to Peaka

Now that you have your requirements, you can connect it to Peaka using the following steps:

1. Navigate to the settings page in Peaka.

2. Click the "Add First Connection" button to initiate the connection process.

3. In the modal that appears, select "Twilio" as your connection type.

4. Enter your connection name, SID and Auth Token.

<img src="https://cdn.peaka.com/integrations/twilio-3.png" />

5. Click "Authorize" to create your Twilio connection.

<img src="https://cdn.peaka.com/integrations/twilio-2.png" />

Congratulations! You've successfully created a connection between Twilio and Peaka. You can now access and manage your Twilio data seamlessly within the Peaka platform.
