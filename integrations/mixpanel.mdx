This article guides you through the process of connecting Mixpanel to Peaka, allowing you to seamlessly access your Mixpanel data within the Peaka platform.

## Prerequisites

Before you start, you will need the following information from Mixpanel.

- Username
- Secret
- Project ID
- Region (Optional)

Follow the steps below to obtain it:

1. Enter your Mixpanel username for username field.

2. Navigate to your Mixpanel project settings page to locate your Secret, Region, and Project ID credentials as illustrated in the screenshots below.

<img src="https://cdn.peaka.com/integrations/mixpanel-1.png" />

<img src="https://cdn.peaka.com/integrations/mixpanel-2.png" />

## Connecting Mixpanel to Peaka

1. Navigate to the data model page in Peaka.

2. In the side menu bar, click the "New Data Source" button to initiate the connection process.

3. In the modal that appears, select "Mixpanel" as your data source.

4. Enter a name for your data source and input the information you obtained in the previous step.

<img src="https://cdn.peaka.com/integrations/mixpanel-3.png" />

5. Click "Connect" to create your Mixpanel data source.

Congratulations! You've successfully connected Mixpanel to Peaka. You can now access and manage your Mixpanel data seamlessly within the Peaka platform.
