This article guides you through the process of connecting Mailchimp Marketing to Peaka, allowing you to seamlessly access your Mailchimp Marketing data within the Peaka platform.

## Prerequisites

Follow the steps below to connect it.

1. Go to the settings page on Peaka.

2. Click the "Add First Connection" button. This will open a modal.

3. In the modal, select "Mailchimp Marketing" as your connection type.

4. Enter a name for your connection and click the "Signing in to Mailchimp Marketing" button.

<img src="https://cdn.peaka.com/integrations/marketing-integration-1.png" />

5. Once clicking, it will open a modal for logging in to your Mailchimp account.

<img src="https://cdn.peaka.com/integrations/marketing-integration-2.png" />

6. In the Mailchimp login modal, enter your Mailchimp credentials

7. After logging in, you will be asked to allow Peaka access to your Mailchimp Marketing account. Click the "Allow" button to proceed.

<img src="https://cdn.peaka.com/integrations/marketing-integration-3.png" />

Congratulations! You've successfully connected Mailchimp Marketing to Peaka. You can now access and manage your Jira data seamlessly within the Peaka platform.
