This article guides you through the process of connecting Excel 365 to Peaka, allowing you to seamlessly access your Excel 365 data within the Peaka platform.

## Connecting Excel 365 to Peaka

1. Navigate to the data model page in Peaka.

2. Click the "Bring your data" button to initiate the connection process.

3. In the modal that appears, select "Excel 365" as your connection type.

4. Enter a name for your connection.

<img src="https://cdn.peaka.com/integrations/excel365-1.png" />

5. Click the "Sign in" button to log in with your Excel 365 account. A pop-up window will appear, redirecting you to the Excel 365 login page.

6. Click the "Accept" button which will create your Excel 365 connection.

Congratulations! You've successfully connected Excel 365 to Peaka. You can now access and manage your Excel 365 data seamlessly within the Peaka platform.
