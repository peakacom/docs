This article guides you through the process of connecting Dynamics 365 to Peaka, allowing you to seamlessly access your Dynamics 365 data within the Peaka platform.

## Prerequisites

Before you start, you will need the following information from Dynamics 365.

- Dynamics 365 URL
- Client ID (required for advanced authentication)
- Client Secret (required for advanced authentication)

Follow the steps below to obtain it:

1. To obtain the Dynamics 365 URL, refer to this [article](https://community.dynamics.com/blogs/post/?postid=d1ebf5d8-0a0c-41af-91ff-cd25f1cb37f7).

2. To obtain the Client ID and Client Secret, refer to this [article](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/dynamics365/finance/localizations/global/universal-tax-rate-api-how-to-setup-clientid-and-clientsecret).

## Connecting Dynamics 365 to Peaka

Now that you have obtained the necessary integration data and credentials, you can connect Dynamics 365 to Peaka using the following steps:

1. Navigate to the settings page in Peaka.

2. Click the "Bring your data" button to initiate the connection process:

3. In the modal that appears, select "Dynamics 365" as your connection type.

4. Enter a name for your connection, input the credentials you obtained in the previous steps.

<img src="https://cdn.peaka.com/integrations/dynamics365-1.png" />

5. Click "Sign in" to create your Dynamics 365 connection.

Congratulations! You've successfully connected Dynamics 365 to Peaka. You can now access and manage your Dynamics 365 data seamlessly within the Peaka platform.
