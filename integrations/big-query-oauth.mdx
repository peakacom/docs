This article guides you through the process of connecting BigQuery (Oauth) to Peaka, allowing you to seamlessly access your BigQuery data within the Peaka platform.

## Prerequisites

Before you start, you will need the following information from BigQuery (Oauth).

- Project ID

Follow the steps below to obtain it:

1. Go to the BigQuery dashboard. You can find your Project ID in the blue squared area.

<img src="https://cdn.peaka.com/integrations/big-query-oauth-1.png" />

## Connecting BigQuery (Oauth) to Peaka

Now that you have obtained the necessary integration data and credentials, you can connect BigQuery (Oauth) to Peaka using the following steps:

1. Navigate to the settings page in Peaka.

2. Click the "Add First Connection" button to initiate the connection process:

3. In the modal that appears, select "BigQuery (Oauth)" as your connection type.

4. Enter a name for your connection and enter obtained project id..

<img src="https://cdn.peaka.com/integrations/big-query-oauth-2.png" />

5. Click "Sign in" button sign in with your BigQuery account.

6. Click the "Allow" button which will create your BigQuery (Oauth) connection.

<img src="https://cdn.peaka.com/integrations/big-query-oauth-3.png" />

Congratulations! You've successfully connected BigQuery (Oauth) to Peaka. You can now access and manage your BigQuery (Oauth) data seamlessly within the Peaka platform.
