This article guides you through the process of connecting Zoom to Peaka, allowing you to seamlessly access your Zoom data within the Peaka platform.

## Prerequisites

Before you start, you will need the following information from Zoom.

- Client ID (required for advanced authentication)
- Client Secret (required for advanced authentication)

Follow the steps below to obtain it:

1. Navigate to the [Zoom Marketplace](https://marketplace.zoom.us/) site and click 'Manage' from the top right corner of the screen.

<img src="https://cdn.peaka.com/integrations/zoom-1.png" />

2. Select your app and then click the 'App Credentials' tab from the left panel. Use the values for the 'Client ID' and 'Client secret' available on this page.

<img src="https://cdn.peaka.com/integrations/zoom-2.png" />

## Connecting Zoom to Peaka

Now that you have obtained the necessary integration data and credentials, you can connect Zoom to Peaka using the following steps:

1. Navigate to the data model page in Peaka.

2. Click the "Bring your data" button to initiate the connection process.

3. In the modal that appears, select "Zoom" as your connection type.

4. Enter a name for your connection, input the credentials you obtained in the previous steps.

<img src="https://cdn.peaka.com/integrations/zoom-3.png" />

5. Click "Sign in" to create your Zoom connection.

Congratulations! You've successfully connected Zoom to Peaka. You can now access and manage your Zoom data seamlessly within the Peaka platform.
