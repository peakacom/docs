This article guides you through the process of connecting HubSpot to Peaka, allowing you to seamlessly access your HubSpot data within the Peaka platform.

## Connecting HubSpot to Peaka

1. Go to the data model page on Peaka.

2. Click the "Bring your data" button. This will open a modal.

3. In the modal, select "HubSpot" as your connection type.

4. Enter a name for your connection name, click the Sign In button. This will open a modal to allow Peaka access to your HubSpot account.

5. Allow Peaka access to your HubSpot account.

<img src="https://cdn.peaka.com/integrations/hubspot-1.png" />

Congratulations! You've successfully connected HubSpot to Peaka. You can now access and manage your HubSpot data seamlessly within the Peaka platform.
