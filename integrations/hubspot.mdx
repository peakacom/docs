This article guides you through the process of connecting HubSpot to Peaka, allowing you to seamlessly access your HubSpot data within the Peaka platform.

Follow these steps to connect HubSpot to Peaka:

1. Go to the settings page on Peaka.

2. Click the "Add First Connection" button. This will open a modal.

3. In the modal, select "HubSpot" as your connection type.

4. Enter a name for your connection name and click the "Signing in to HubSpot" button. This will open a modal to allow Peaka access to your HubSpot account.

<img src="https://cdn.peaka.com/integrations/hubspot-integration-1.png" />

5. In the modal, click the "Choose Account" button to authorize the connection to your HubSpot account.

<img src="https://cdn.peaka.com/integrations/hubspot-integration-2.png" />

Congratulations! You've successfully connected HubSpot to Peaka. You can now access and manage your HubSpot data seamlessly within the Peaka platform.
