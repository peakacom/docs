This article guides you through the process of connecting Hubspot to Peaka, allowing you to seamlessly access your Hubspot data within the Peaka platform.

Hubspot connector provides two authentication types:

- OAuth
- Private App Access Token

## Prerequisites

Before you start, you will need the following information from Hubspot.

- OAuth

  - Client ID (required for advanced authentication)
  - Client Secret (required for advanced authentication)

- Private App Access Token

Follow the steps below to obtain it:

### OAuth

1. To obtain Client ID and Client Secret, refer to this [article](https://developers.hubspot.com/docs/api/working-with-oauth#initiating-an-integration-with-oauth-2-0).

### Private App Access Token

1. To obtain Private App Access Token, refer to this [article](https://developers.hubspot.com/docs/api/private-apps#make-api-calls-with-your-app-s-access-token).

## Connecting HubSpot to Peaka

1. Go to the data model page on Peaka.

2. Click the "Bring your data" button. This will open a modal.

3. In the modal, select "HubSpot" as your connection type.

4. Enter a name for your connection name, select the authentication type and input the credentials you obtained in the previous steps and click the submit button.

<img src="https://cdn.peaka.com/integrations/hubspot-1.png" />

Congratulations! You've successfully connected HubSpot to Peaka. You can now access and manage your HubSpot data seamlessly within the Peaka platform.
