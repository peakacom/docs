This article guides you through the process of connecting Clickhouse to Peaka, allowing you to seamlessly access your Clickhouse data within the Peaka platform.

## Prerequisites

Before you start, you will need the following information from Clickhouse.

- URL
- PORT
- User Name
- Password
- SSL (Toggle "Connect Through TLS" switch)

Follow the steps below to obtain it:

1. Open the Clickhouse dashboard.

2. Click on the "Connect" button as shown in the image below.

<img src="https://cdn.peaka.com/integrations/clickhouse-1.png" />

3. It will open a modal and show the connection detail such as URL, PORT, User Name, and Password.

<img src="https://cdn.peaka.com/integrations/clickhouse-2.png" />

## Connecting Clickhouse to Peaka

Now that you have obtained the necessary integration data and credentials, you can connect Clickhouse to Peaka using the following steps:

1. Navigate to the data model page in Peaka.

2. Click the "Add First Connection" button to initiate the connection process:

3. In the modal that appears, select "Clickhouse" as your connection type.

4. Enter a name for your connection, input the credentials you obtained in the previous steps, and toggle the "Connect Through TLS" switch if SSL is required.

<img src="https://cdn.peaka.com/integrations/clickhouse-3.png" />

5. Click "Next" to create your Clickhouse connection.

Congratulations! You've successfully connected Clickhouse to Peaka. You can now access and manage your Clickhouse data seamlessly within the Peaka platform.
