This article guides you through the process of connecting Mailchimp Transactional to Peaka, allowing you to seamlessly access your Mailchimp Transactional data within the Peaka platform.

## Prerequisites

Before you start, you will need an API Key from Mailchimp Transactional.

Follow the steps below to obtain it:

1. Go to this [link](https://us21.admin.mailchimp.com/account/api/) to create or obtain your Mailchimp Transactional API Key.

2. Click the "Create a Key" button to generate your API Key.

<img src="https://cdn.peaka.com/integrations/transactional-integration-1.png" />

## Connecting Mailchimp Transactional to Peaka

Now that you have your requirements, you can connect it to Peaka using the following steps:

1. Navigate to the settings page in Peaka.

2. Click the "Add First Connection" button to initiate the connection process.

3. In the modal that appears, select "Mailchimp Transactional" as your connection type.

4. Enter your connection name and API Key.

<img src="https://cdn.peaka.com/integrations/transactional-integration-3.png" />

5. Click "Authorize" to create your Mailchimp Transactional connection.

<img src="https://cdn.peaka.com/integrations/transactional-integration-2.png" />

Congratulations! You've successfully created a connection between Mailchimp Transactional and Peaka. You can now access and manage your Mailchimp Transactional data seamlessly within the Peaka platform.
