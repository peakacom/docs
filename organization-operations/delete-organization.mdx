---
title: "Delete Organization"
description: "Learn how to delete an organization in Peaka."
---

This article explains how you can delete an organization in Peaka.

1. Log in to your Peaka account.
2. Click on the three-dots button (ellipsis icon) associated with the organization and click on the "**Settings**" option.

<img
  src="https://cdn.peaka.com/organization-operations/organization-menu.png"
  alt="organization menu"
/>

3. Click on the "**Delete Organization**" button.

<img
  src="https://cdn.peaka.com/organization-operations/organization-info.png"
  alt="organization information"
/>

4. Enter the organization name and click on the "**Delete**" button.

<img
  src="https://cdn.peaka.com/organization-operations/delete-organization-1.png"
  alt="delete organization"
/>

That's it! Your organization will be deleted.
