---
title: "Manage Organization Groups"
description: "Learn how to manage organization groups in Peaka."
---

This article explains how you can manage organization groups in Peaka.

### Accessing Organization Groups

1. Log in to your Peaka account.
2. Click on the three-dots button (ellipsis icon) associated with the organization and click on the "**Settings**" option.

<img
  src="https://cdn.peaka.com/organization-operations/organization-menu.png"
  alt="organization menu"
/>

3. Click on the "**Groups**" tab on the tabs list.

<img
  src="https://cdn.peaka.com/organization-operations/organization-group.png"
  alt="organization groups"
/>

### Creating a new group

1. Click on the "**Create Group**" button on the top right corner of the page or center of the page if you don't have any groups.

<img
  src="https://cdn.peaka.com/organization-operations/organization-group.png"
  alt="organization groups"
/>

2. Enter the group name, description and select the members you want to add to the group. Then click on the "**Create**" button.

<img
  src="https://cdn.peaka.com/organization-operations/create-group.png"
  alt="create group"
/>

That's it. The group will be created.

### Updating group name

1. Click the three-dots button (ellipsis icon) associated with the group and click on the "**Rename**" option.

<img
  src="https://cdn.peaka.com/organization-operations/group-menu.png"
  alt="group menu"
/>

2. Enter the new group name in inline edit mode and click on the "Enter" key to confirm the change.

<img
  src="https://cdn.peaka.com/organization-operations/rename-group.png"
  alt="rename group in inline edit mode"
/>

That's it. The group name will be updated.

### Delete a group

1. Click the three-dots button (ellipsis icon) associated with the group and click on the "**Delete**" option.

<img
  src="https://cdn.peaka.com/organization-operations/group-menu.png"
  alt="group menu"
/>

2. Click on the "**Delete**" button on the confirmation modal.

<img
  src="https://cdn.peaka.com/organization-operations/delete-group.png"
  alt="delete group in confirmation modal"
/>

That's it. The group will be deleted.

### Add a member to a group

1. Click on the **Add Member** button on the associated group.

<img
  src="https://cdn.peaka.com/organization-operations/add-member-to-group-1.png"
  alt="add member to group"
/>

2. Select the member you want to add to the group and click on the "**Add Member**" button.

<img
  src="https://cdn.peaka.com/organization-operations/add-member-to-group-2.png"
  alt="add member to group"
/>

That's it. The member will be added to the group.

### Remove a member from a group

1. Click the three-dots button (ellipsis icon) associated with the group member and click on the "**Remove Member**" option.

<img
  src="https://cdn.peaka.com/organization-operations/group-member-menu.png"
  alt="group member menu"
/>

2. Click on the "**Remove Member**" button on the confirmation modal.

<img
  src="https://cdn.peaka.com/organization-operations/remove-group-member.png"
  alt="remove group member"
/>

That's it. The member will be removed from the group.
