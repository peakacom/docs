---
title: "Manage Organization Members"
description: "Learn how to manage organization members in Peaka."
---

This article explains how you can manage organization members in Peaka.

### Accessing Organization Members

1. Log in to your Peaka account.
2. Click on the three-dots button (ellipsis icon) associated with the organization and click on the "**Settings**" option.

<img
  src="https://cdn.peaka.com/organization-operations/organization-menu.png"
  alt="organization menu"
/>

3. Click on the "**Organization Members**" tab on the tabs list.

<img
  src="https://cdn.peaka.com/organization-operations/organization-members.png"
  alt="manage organization members"
/>

### Inviting a member to the organization

1. Click on the "**Invite Member**" button on the top right corner of the page.

2. Enter the email address of the member you want to add and select their adding type (Workspace / Group), select roles and add message if you want to. Then click on the "**Invite**" button.

<img
  src="https://cdn.peaka.com/organization-operations/invite-member-modal.png"
  alt="invite member"
/>

That's it. After accepting the invitation, the member will be added to the organization.

### Cancelling an invitation

1. After inviting a member, you can cancel the invitation. Click on the "**Pending Invitations**" tab to see the pending invitations.

2. Click on the three-dots button (ellipsis icon) associated with the invitation and click on the "**Cancel**" option.

<img
  src="https://cdn.peaka.com/organization-operations/pending-invitations-1.png"
  alt="cancel invitation"
/>

3. Click on the "**Cancel**" button on the confirmation modal.

<img
  src="https://cdn.peaka.com/organization-operations/pending-invitations-2.png"
  alt="cancel invitation confirmation"
/>

That's it. The invitation will be cancelled.

### Updating a member's role

1. Click on the member's role to open the role selection dropdown and select the new role.

<img
  src="https://cdn.peaka.com/organization-operations/update-member-role.png"
  alt="update member role"
/>

That's it. The member's role will be updated.

### Updating a member's Group

Note: If the member is added to a group, you can update the group of the member.

1. Click on the member's group to open the group selection dropdown and select the new group.

<img
  src="https://cdn.peaka.com/organization-operations/update-member-group.png"
  alt="update member group"
/>

That's it. The member's group will be updated.

### Removing a member from the organization

1. Click on the three-dots button (ellipsis icon) associated with the member and click on the "**Remove**" option.

<img
  src="https://cdn.peaka.com/organization-operations/remove-member.png"
  alt="remove member"
/>

2. Click on the "**Remove**" button on the confirmation modal.

<img
  src="https://cdn.peaka.com/organization-operations/remove-member-confirmation.png"
  alt="remove member confirmation"
/>

That's it. The member will be removed from the organization.
