---
title: "Leave Organization"
description: "Learn how to leave an organization in Peaka."
---

This article explains how you can leave an organization in Peaka.

1. Log in to your Peaka account.

2. Click on the three-dots button (ellipsis icon) next to the organization you want to leave and select the **Leave Organization** option. Alternatively, navigate to **Organization Settings** and click the **Leave Organization** button in the top-right corner.

<img
  src="https://cdn.peaka.com/organization-operations/leave-organization-1.png"
  alt="leave organization option in the organization menu"
/>

3. A confirmation modal will appear. Follow the steps based on your role in the organization:
   - **If you are the owner:** Select a new owner from the **Transfer ownership to** dropdown and click the **Transfer & Leave** button.

   <img
     src="https://cdn.peaka.com/organization-operations/leave-organization-3.png"
     alt="transfer ownership and leave modal for organization owners"
   />
   - **If you are an admin or member:** Click the **Leave** button to confirm.

   <img
     src="https://cdn.peaka.com/organization-operations/leave-organization-2.png"
     alt="leave organization confirmation modal for admins and members"
   />

You have successfully left the organization.
