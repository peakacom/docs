---
title: "Create Organization"
description: "Learn how to create an organization in Peaka."
---

This article explains how you can create an organization in Peaka.

1. Log in to your Peaka account.
2. Click on the "**Create Organization**" button.

<img
  src="https://cdn.peaka.com/organization-operations/create-organization-1.png"
  alt="create organization"
/>

3. Enter organization name and click on the "**Next**" button.

<img
  src="https://cdn.peaka.com/organization-operations/create-organization-2.png"
  alt="organization page"
/>

4. If you want to add members to your organization, enter their email addresses and select their role from the dropdown menu and click on the "**Next**" button. If you don't want to add members, click on the "**Skip for now**" button.

<img
  src="https://cdn.peaka.com/organization-operations/create-organization-3.png"
  alt="add members"
/>

5. Your organization will be created and click on the "**Finish**" button to go to the organization page.

<img
  src="https://cdn.peaka.com/organization-operations/create-organization-4.png"
  alt="organization page"
/>
