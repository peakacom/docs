This article explains you can create different types of flows in Peaka.

## Creating a Flow for a Component

To create a flow for a specific component, follow these steps:

1. Click on the target component.

2. Click on the 'Show Flow' button.

<img
  src="https://cdn.peaka.com/flows/how-to-create-flows-1.png"
  alt="peaka button component added view"
/>

3. Next, click on the 'Create a new flow' button to generate a flow for your component. You can then proceed to add action nodes to the flow.

<img
  src="https://cdn.peaka.com/flows/how-to-create-flows-2.png"
  alt="shows a flow of added button component"
/>

## Creating Reusable or Backend Flows

If you wish to create a reusable or backend flow, follow these steps:

1. Before clicking on the 'Show Flow' button, ensure that no component is selected. Click on the white area of the designer canvas to deselect any currently selected component.

<img
  src="https://cdn.peaka.com/flows/how-to-create-flows-3.png"
  alt="closing the flow of added button component"
/>

2. Locate the plus icon, as shown in the image below, and click on it.

<img
  src="https://cdn.peaka.com/flows/how-to-create-flows-4.png"
  alt="open resuable and backend function flow"
/>

3. A modal window will appear. You can now choose between 'Reusable Flow' or 'Backend Flow.' Select the appropriate option, enter a name for your flow, and click the 'Create Flow' button.

<img
  src="https://cdn.peaka.com/flows/how-to-create-flows-5.png"
  alt="adding a reuseable flow modal"
/>

Your flow will be successfully created.

<img
  src="https://cdn.peaka.com/flows/how-to-create-flows-6.png"
  alt="created successfull of reuseable flow"
/>
